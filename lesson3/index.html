Purpose


The purpose of this website is to inform people about the weather to help protect life, property, and the economy of the World. Whether staying home or going out on a vacation it is important for people to plan accordantly to the weather circumstances, not doing so could cause accidents, sickness, or even death. Both private and public property need to be taken care off according to weather forecasts, this help preserve the properties for a longer period of time and costs less in the long run - preventing to have to rebuild the property again from scratch. Lastly, yet very important, the weather forecasts help to improve the global economy for companies can plan to operate according to the weather and guarantee the business success.
