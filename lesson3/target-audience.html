Target Audience

Everyone is welcome to visit the site, however, for the purpose of having a target audience, the audience will most likely be adults over 25 years old. The gender of our audience will both be male and female. The income of our audience would most likely be over 50,000 a year for they will own a house, vehicle, business, or be planning a family/business trip. The target audience would be people in the United States of America, for the site language will be in American-English, however, people from around the globe is welcome to visit our site.

Two personas

The main two personas the site has in mind are the workers and the self-employees. The workers are those who work in a company and commute to work every day and may do business trips. The business owners are those who own a company and needs to rely on weather circumstances to properly run the business to avoid losing income or the business asset.

Five Scenarios

Scenario 1: A family would like to plan a family vacation. Weather 4 Real will allow the family to plan accordingly to the destination's weather. Planning accordingly involves knowing when is the best time to visit the destination, what clothe to wear, and what places to visit and at what time.
Scenario 2: A person planning a business trip. Knowing the weather will help plan accordingly for flights are safe and not delayed because of weather circumstances. It will also help the person traveling to plan on what clothe to bring and what weather to expect ones at the destination.
Scenario 3: A business owner running the business all year around. Knowing whether there is a major climate change coming up or a minor rain helps the business owners plan accordingly so the business does not close and keeps running and generating an income.
Scenario 4: Retirement. Many people when retired wants to life in near their children/grandchildren or would like to move to different countries and experience different life styles. Weather 4 Real help people know whether or not the destination they are thinking to retire on is ideal for them and their health.
Scenario 5: Daily life activities. Lastly, Weather 4 Real will help people plan their daily routines accordingly, avoiding getting sick or running into accidents.
