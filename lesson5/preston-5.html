Article

BOISE, Idaho — Just looking at the temperatures we've seen in Boise so far this year, we really could call this a "warm winter." Through January and February (so far), there were 8 days with high temps at 50° or warmer, and only 5 days with high temps in the 30s. So far this year, only two days had below average high temps.

For Tuesday, look for mostly sunny skies (clouds to start, with clearing expected). Treasure Valley high temps will be in the mid 40s. It's a similar story for Wednesday, with valley highs approaching 50° once again.

Yes, you read that right, there's a good chance that the Treasure Valley will see some snow accumulation later this week and this weekend. It's an active pattern that continues to bring rounds of moisture in our direction for a few days. High temperatures for the valleys will be in the 30s -- below average, for a change.--KTVB7
